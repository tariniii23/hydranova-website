Meet the Team

The students behind HydraNova’s vision for sustainable aviation.

Tarini Supekar

Co-Founder | Sustainability & Systems Lead

Tarini is deeply passionate about sustainability, clean energy, and solving real-world environmental problems through innovation. She played a key role in shaping the overall vision of HydraNova 1.0, ensuring that the aircraft balances environmental responsibility with practicality for commercial airlines. With a strong interest in science and global issues like climate change, Tarini focuses on how hydrogen technology, AI-assisted flight systems, and smart design choices can work together to reduce aviation’s carbon footprint. She believes that meaningful change happens when technology is designed not just to impress, but to make a measurable positive impact on the planet.

Ashvath Karnad

Co-Founder | Design & Technology Lead

Ashvath is passionate about aerospace design, future technologies, and innovative problem-solving. He led the technical and design-focused aspects of HydraNova 1.0, helping translate ambitious sustainability goals into a realistic aircraft concept. With a keen interest in aircraft efficiency, modular systems, and emerging propulsion technologies, Ashvath worked on ideas such as modular hydrogen storage, lightweight composite materials, and quiet electric propulsion. He is motivated by the challenge of making advanced technology accessible and scalable, especially in industries like aviation that have traditionally been hard to decarbonise.
